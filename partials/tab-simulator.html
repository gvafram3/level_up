Live CWA Simulator

Use the live simulator below to enter your courses, credit hours, and scores. It calculates your Semester Weighted Average and Cumulative Weighted Average in real time, flags trails, and shows the impact of resit scores across all your years.

📌

How to use: Enter your actual score out of 100 for each course. The grade (A/B/C/D/F) is derived automatically. Any score below 40 is a trail — a Resit Semester will appear automatically for that year. Open in full screen for the best experience.
