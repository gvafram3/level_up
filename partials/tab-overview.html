Programme Overview

The Five-Day Plan

A structured seminar built for KNUST undergraduate students across all programmes. Each day targets a real academic challenge — not theory, but actionable knowledge students can apply immediately.

Day 1 — Understanding Your CWA

How the KNUST CWA formula works, what drives it, how to calculate it, and how to set a realistic target. Students use the live simulator on this page.

Day 2 — Trails & Academic Recovery

What trails are, how they compound across semesters, and a practical trail recovery plan. The simulator shows real CWA impact with and without resit grades.

Day 3 — AI for Study (Not Just Submission)

Claude, ChatGPT, NotebookLM, quiz platforms — with practical prompts for any course. Includes KNUST’s own AI 150 course and responsible AI use.

Day 4 — MS Word & Digital Organisation

Word essentials every student must know: styles, references, TOC, PDF export, and a file system that saves hours per semester.

Day 5 — Preparing for Next Semester

How to consciously apply everything from this seminar going into the next semester. Study timetable, past questions strategy, and the digital Semester Contract — the most important task of the week.

💡

How to Use This Resource

Navigate using the tabs above. The CWA Simulator tab is fully interactive — students can enter their own scores across three semesters and see live CWA calculations, trail detection, resit impact, and projections.
